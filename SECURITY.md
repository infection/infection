# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Infection is not yet stable. As such, only the latest version is supported.
However, older versions may be patched depending on the severity of the vulnerability.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please do not publicly disclose security vulnerabilities.

If you discover something that may be a vulnerability, please
[report it **privately** on GitHub][vulnerability-report].

* Go to the [Security and Quality][infection-security] tab in the infection repository.
* Click **Report a vulnerability** and complete the form with as much information as possible.
* Submit the report, and we will look into it as soon as possible.

Thank you for responsibly disclosing issues in Infection.
